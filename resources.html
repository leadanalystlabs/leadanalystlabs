// 3 resources

Free Veterans
Boots to Books

A nonprofit providing no-cost assistance to veterans, transitioning military members, and military families in attaining cybersecurity education and employment. Offers free access to Google's Cybersecurity Certificate, up to $1,000 in certification vouchers (CompTIA, etc.), monthly live events, and job placement connections. If you served, this is a must-know resource.

Veterans Cert Vouchers Job Placement Military Spouses
Nonprofit / Scholarships / Events Visit Boots to Books

Free Veterans
ACT Now Education

A nonprofit founded by a Marine veteran serving 77,000+ transitioning service members, veterans, and spouses. Provides free certifications, career resources, career advancement mentorship, and structured pathways from military service into civilian cybersecurity careers. The founder received the Spirit of Hope Award for this work.

Veterans Free Certs Mentorship Transition
Nonprofit / Training / Mentorship Visit ACT Now
🌐
Free Veterans
CyberSkills2Work — National Program

A federally funded national cybersecurity workforce development program led by the University of West Florida and backed by the NSA and DoD — but delivered through a coalition of 10 NCAE-C institutions across the country. Completely free for transitioning military, first responders, veterans, military spouses, and government personnel. Each institution offers its own training pathways for roles like Cyber Defense Analyst, System Administrator, and AI/ML Specialist — with full cert vouchers (Security+, CySA+, etc.), digital badges, and job placement support. Florida residents can also access the UWF-specific CyberSkills2Work Florida pathway listed below.

Cyber Florida at USF — CyberWorks

The Florida Center for Cybersecurity at the University of South Florida runs multiple free programs for Florida residents. CyberWorks is their flagship multi-week hands-on training program preparing students for Tier 1 Cyber Analyst roles (avg. starting salary ~$111K). Also includes FirstLine (free public-sector training) and the ARCS Range (free cyber range for state employees).

Free Florida
CyberSkills2Work Florida (UWF)

The University of West Florida's Florida-specific arm of CyberSkills2Work. Offers free, asynchronous cybersecurity training pathways for eligible Florida residents — covering System Administrator, Cyber Defense Analyst, and AI/ML Specialist roles. Includes certification prep for CompTIA A+, Network+, and Security+, all at zero cost.

// 1 resource

Free Trial OT / ICS Hands-On
Labshock — OT Security Lab

A hands-on OT and ICS security learning platform that spins up a realistic industrial control system environment using Docker — including PLCs, SCADA, real protocols like Modbus TCP, and a built-in IDS. Free trial mode available. Perfect for anyone wanting to understand how critical infrastructure attacks actually work in a safe, isolated lab. Bridges the gap between IT and OT security.

ICS / SCADA Modbus Docker Lab Critical Infra
Virtual Lab Platform Visit Labshock

Why OT/ICS Matters for CTI

As a former Marine Science Technician who worked maritime security — including facilities tied to critical infrastructure — understanding how adversaries target Operational Technology is a natural extension of your background. OT/ICS threat intelligence is a specialized, high-demand niche. Resources like Labshock and Dragos OT-CERT (also free) are excellent starting points.
